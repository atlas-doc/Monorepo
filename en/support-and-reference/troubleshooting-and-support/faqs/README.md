### FAQ

#### Where should a new Atlas API partner start?

Start with [Before Integration](/broken/spaces/6LsKtmbJhZxgxraY5mHB/pages/atRqZqfIII5j9gLe0jRu) for high-priority planning questions.

Then use [Kickoff Checklist](/broken/spaces/6LsKtmbJhZxgxraY5mHB/pages/wrBKOpzwnb7ki3On0sXx) to align flow choice, payment design, ticketing handling, and launch scope.

#### Where can I find booking and ticketing questions?

Use [Search & Booking](/broken/spaces/6LsKtmbJhZxgxraY5mHB/pages/XrP8GBROs9JgYdkjYKPj) for search, verify, pricing, and booking flow questions.

Use [Orders & Ticketing](/broken/spaces/6LsKtmbJhZxgxraY5mHB/pages/lbLT2qkyVzsb5Mak1Kpg) for order status, ticketing, PNR, and follow-up questions.

#### Where can I find payment or refund questions?

Use [Payments](/broken/spaces/6LsKtmbJhZxgxraY5mHB/pages/I4apn7RVlP6yFkpJazm3) for deposit, VCC, card handling, and payment limits.

Use [Post-booking](/broken/spaces/6LsKtmbJhZxgxraY5mHB/pages/uvnvVffeDxSurkQpIoj0) and [Finance](/broken/spaces/6LsKtmbJhZxgxraY5mHB/pages/tK6nC3S4gDNT3KmHMwQh) for refunds, follow-up, top-ups, and reconciliation questions.
